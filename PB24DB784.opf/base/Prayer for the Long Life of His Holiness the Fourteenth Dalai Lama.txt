Prayer for the Long Life of His Holiness the Fourteenth Dalai Lama
by Kyabje Trulshik Rinpoche

jetsün jampel ngakgi wangpö ku
With the enlightened form of noble Mañjuśrī, Lord of Speech,
nyingjé terchen lodrö zangpö sung
Enlightened speech of the great treasury of compassion, excellent in intellect,
yeshe tutop sangwé dakpö tuk
And enlightened mind of the wise and powerful Lord of Secrets,
gyalwé wangpo tendzin gyatsö dé
Lord of the victorious ones, Tenzin Gyatso, holder of all the teachings,
yong dzok tenpé dakpor shyap ten söl
We pray that your life may remain secure!
